Annex A – Non-comprehensive list of self-identified G7 actions for extreme weather prediction, preparedness, and response

This annex compiles initiatives from G7 members and partners under two main themes: (1) Emerging Technologies for Weather Prediction, Preparedness, and Response; and (2) Deploying Nature-based Solutions and Other Effective Approaches for Building Resilience to Extreme Weather.
