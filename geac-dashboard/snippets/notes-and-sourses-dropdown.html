Notes and Sources – Sample Indicators

Education

Indicator 1 – OECD PISA Mathematics Scores

Measures the mathematical literacy of 15-year-olds to apply and interpret mathematics in various contexts. Scores are scaled to a mean of 500 and a standard deviation of 100. This indicator indirectly relates to SDG 4.1.1.

Source: OECD (2024), Mathematics performance (PISA).

Indicator 2 – Tertiary Education and Women in STEM
  • 2a. Share of the population aged 25–64 with tertiary education (ISCED 5–8).
  • 2b. Share of women among all tertiary graduates in STEM fields.

Both sub-indicators relate to SDG 4.5.1.

Sources: OECD Data Explorer – Population with tertiary education; Share of graduates by gender in STEM.

Employment and Social Security

Indicator 3 – Labour Force Participation and Part-Time Work
  • 3a. Labour force participation rate (15–64).
  • 3b. Share of women among all part-time employees (<30 hours).
  • 3c. Share of women in STEM jobs.

These indicators indirectly relate to SDG 8.5.

Source: OECD Data Explorer – Labour force participation; EU Labour Force Survey.
